Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about sliding door repair in Central Florida. Don't see yours? Just give us a call.

How much does a sliding door repair cost?

We give you flat-rate pricing right over the phone — no hidden fees, no surprises, and no upcharges once we arrive. Because we specialize only in sliding doors, we can usually quote your repair before we ever leave the shop. Call (352) 234-4353 and you'll speak directly with the person who's going to fix your door.

Can you really fix any sliding glass door?

Yes — we repair all makes and models of residential sliding glass doors. Rollers that stick or jump the track, worn or bent tracks, broken locks and handles, and cracked or fogged glass are everyday work for us. Most doors people assume need replacing just need the right parts and an expert touch.

How fast can you come out?

We carry every common part on the truck, so most repairs are finished in a single visit — often the same week you call. Reach us Monday–Friday, 8am–5pm at (352) 234-4353 and we'll get you on the schedule.

Do I need a whole new door, or can it be repaired?

In most cases, repair. Sticking and hard-to-slide doors are almost always a roller or track problem — not a reason to replace the entire door. We'll always tell you honestly when a repair makes sense and when a new door is the better value (we install those too).

What areas do you serve?

We cover all of Central Florida from our Eustis, FL home base — The Villages, Ocala, Orlando, Daytona Beach, Melbourne, and everywhere in between. We also keep sliding doors working for property managers, landlords, and HOAs across the region.
