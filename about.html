About Us

A family-owned business built on honest work, fair pricing, and a passion for sliding glass door repair. We get very picky about making things work perfectly!

Our Story

Father and Son team that have seen every single door problem in the history of sliding glass doors

The long journey to 40 years of experience begins in 1986 with Jim getting a phone call at 20 years of age when he worked at Fleetwood Aluminum, a major door manufacturer. On the phone was a Fleetwood customer that was constantly impressed by Jim's service and he offered Jim a job with his existing sliding door repair and installation business. The rest became glass door history!

After a long training period, Jim was sent out to expand the operation as an unofficial franchise and what started as a one-man, one-van operation in 1987 grew into the largest sliding door repair company in Southern California. We now have these unofficial franchise locations across many states as our employees have moved over the decades and we help them get going.

Joshua joined the team in 2006 when he turned 18 and became the lead technician for all branches by the time he was 22. In 2008, the father and son dynamic duo moved to Florida, leaving the California division in the hands of Shaun (657) 360-4866 the son-in-law. If you want to hear the experience level for yourself, call Jim today at (352) 234-4353 and just listen!
