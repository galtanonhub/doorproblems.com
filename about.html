About Us

A family-owned business built on honest work, fair pricing, and a passion for keeping Florida homes safe and comfortable.

Our Story

Started in a Garage. Now Central Florida's Go-To Door Experts.

DoorProblems.com was founded in 2008 by Gary and Linda Marsh after Gary spent 12 years working for a major window and door manufacturer. Tired of watching homeowners get overcharged by companies that didn't specialize in door repair, Gary started a business with a simple promise: show up on time, do quality work, and charge a fair price.

What started as a one-van operation out of their garage in The Villages has grown into a team of eight technicians serving four counties across Central Florida — but the values have never changed.

Today, Gary and Linda's son Derek runs day-to-day operations alongside a team that collectively has over 60 years of hands-on door experience. Every technician is background-checked, drug-tested, and trained specifically on sliding door systems.
