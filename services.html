Glass Replacement

Cracked, shattered, or cloudy (thermopane failure) glass panels reduce security and comfort. We install replacement glass panels on-site using safety-grade tempered glass that meets Florida building codes. Ask about our secret lower cost option!

We also offer upgrades to Low-E, impact-resistant, and privacy glass options that can reduce your energy bills and improve storm protection.

  • Broken or cracked glass panels
  • Fogged or cloudy double-pane glass (seal failure)

  • Impact-resistant glass upgrades
  • Low-E energy-efficient glass
  • Privacy and tinted glass options

No More Loud Grinding and Bumping

A worn or damaged track has only a minimal effect on the ease with which a sliding glass door can move back and forth, but the quality of life difference can be huge. If you see what we call "roller coaster" like bumps or indentations in your track, we will make the door slide easily but when it hits those bumps it will rattle the door and make some unpleasant noises.

We carry track replacement parts with us at all times and come prepared for this issue as it is relatively common. They are rarely necessary but can be highly desired for optimal use so we have you covered.

New Door Installation

Do NOT let somebody tell you that your door MUST be replaced. This is almost never the case as we can repair anything on the door that makes it actually function. Locks & Handles

A sliding door with a faulty latch or handle is an entry point for intruders. We repair and replace all types of sliding door hardware, from simple handle sets to high-security multi-point locking systems.

We stock keyed locks, keyless deadbolts, child safety locks, and smart-lock compatible hardware for modern homes.

  • Broken or stiff door handles
  • Faulty latches that won't catch
  • Keyed lock cylinder replacement
  • Multi-point locking system installation
  • Child safety lock add-ons
  • Security bar and auxiliary lock installation
